Porsches first car was actually what we now know as a volkswagen beetle. In 1931 the German Government assigned ferdinand porsche to create a car for the people. After the success of the beetle ferdinand and is son ferry created a their own car. Called the 356 the father and son created a prototype to be shown to local dealerships. The car was put into production in 1950.
